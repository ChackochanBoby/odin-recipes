Grilled Chicken Marinade

Description

Grilled chicken marinade is the secret to transforming a simple chicken dish into a flavorful feast. This easy-to-make marinade infuses the meat with a burst of flavors, ensuring every bite is juicy and delicious. A perfect balance of olive oil, fresh lemon juice, garlic, and a blend of herbs and spices like rosemary, thyme, paprika, and black pepper, works wonders to tenderize the chicken while adding a delightful zest. Whether you're preparing for a summer barbecue or a quick weeknight dinner, marinating your chicken for at least a few hours—or overnight for the best results—will elevate its taste and tenderness. Explore our collection of grilled chicken marinade recipes to find the perfect match for your next grilling adventure.

Ingredients

  • Red wine vinegar: This top-rated grilled chicken marinade starts with sweet, tangy, and flavorful red wine vinegar.
  • Oil: Aromatic olive oil infuses the meat with flavor and moisture, ensuring deliciously juicy chicken every time.
  • Soy sauce: This grilled chicken marinade calls for low sodium soy sauce, which we think delivers the perfect amount of saltiness. Of course, if you like your marinade extra salty, you can use regular soy sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This savory grilled chicken marinade calls for these earthy, herbaceous spices and seasonings:
    • Dried parsley flakes
    • Dried basil
    • Dried oregano
    • Garlic powder
    • Ground black pepper
