Grilled Chicken Marinade

Description

Grilled chicken marinade is the secret to transforming a simple chicken dish into a flavorful feast. This easy-to-make marinade infuses the meat with a burst of flavors, ensuring every bite is juicy and delicious. A perfect balance of olive oil, fresh lemon juice, garlic, and a blend of herbs and spices like rosemary, thyme, paprika, and black pepper, works wonders to tenderize the chicken while adding a delightful zest. Whether you're preparing for a summer barbecue or a quick weeknight dinner, marinating your chicken for at least a few hours—or overnight for the best results—will elevate its taste and tenderness.
